This is my blog of my summervacation to valleta, malta.

The people living there

Malta is a pretty small island with about 500,000 people living there. Most of the people are Maltese, but there’s a mix of other ethnic groups too, and it’s getting more diverse as more people move there from different countries. In general, people in Malta are open-minded and welcoming toward others, no matter where they’re from. It’s a tolerant place where people from all kinds of backgrounds are respected and accepted.
